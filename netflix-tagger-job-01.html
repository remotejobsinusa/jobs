About the Job

Netflix is building a new game studio in-house to create and design a new class of social video games. We are looking for a talented UI Artist with a passion for creating beautiful, dynamic visuals that enhance the player experience. The successful candidate will excel at creating beautiful UI assets, menus, HUD elements, and 2D game art, and have a great eye for kinetic motion graphics.

In our small group, making changes is a priority. You will need to create original source art, bring it to life, and work closely with designers and engineers to bring their projects to life. Our ideal candidate is a UX major, capable of creating focused designs with a clean interface and clear user interactions.

Responsibilities

  • Create end-user interface properties that will live on top of our game environment.
  • Animate UI elements using art tools to clearly show the intended rotation and motion schemes.
  • Design graybox UI mocks to block out design and layout.
  • Collaborate with developers to create engaging and intuitive game menus.
  • Work with other teams to improve usability and accessibility.
  • Give and receive creative feedback, set clear creative goals, and define specific outcomes.

Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience as a UI Designer, with at least 1 title presented.
  • Strong knowledge of UX design principles.
  • Experience with Unity, including creating Unity assets and original content.
  • Proficiency with art tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, and After Effects.
  • Strong communication skills, both written and verbal.

Bonus Skills

  • Traditional illustration skills.
  • Experience with video and board games, particularly those found in public arcades.
  • Passionate about your work and creating unforgettable gaming moments.
