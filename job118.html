Job Description

Do you especially need to collaborate and help businesses thrive? At the Apple Store, you partner with business specialists and entrepreneurs to provide the tools they need to put Apple solutions to work in their businesses. As a Business Expert, you help them explore Apple products and discuss potential outcomes. You are part of a team passionate about helping businesses succeed.

As an Apple Support At Home Advisor, you will be assisting customers with a variety of Apple products such as iPhones, iPads, MacBooks, and desktop Macs. As the first point of contact for customers, you’ll be the friendly voice of Apple, providing world-class customer service, troubleshooting, and technical support to ensure a great user experience.

Key Skills and Requirements

  • Knowledge of how businesses use technology and what Apple solutions can offer them.
  • Comfortable using the phone to communicate with clients and discuss business solutions.
  • Ability to work with business insights through in-store briefings, workshops, and events.
  • Ability to provide technical troubleshooting and support for Apple products (iPhones, iPads, MacBooks, etc.).
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Experience working in customer service or related fields is preferred, but not required.
