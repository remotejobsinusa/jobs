Tesla Careers - Remote Job

Date: November 23, 2024

Tesla is looking for a dedicated and passionate Tesla Advisor to provide engaging and informational interactions with both existing and prospective customers regarding the benefits and advantages of Tesla energy products. The Advisor will primarily work with clients in the United States, using various communication channels to address inquiries and support project progression. The role offers the opportunity to become an expert in Tesla’s products and processes while helping to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy.

What You’ll Do

  • Client Interaction: Communicate with both prospective and repeat customers to nurture and prepare them for purchasing Tesla energy products or fulfilling existing orders. Interactions will take place through inbound and outbound calls, SMS, and email.
  • Follow-Up: Respond to inbound requests regarding new orders for Tesla products and existing projects. Identify customer needs quickly and provide resolutions that exceed expectations. Work with internal teams to gather and relay the necessary information.
  • Sales Pipeline Management: Drive a high volume of inbound and outbound calls and activities to meet monthly order and fulfillment targets as well as service level objectives.

Schedule

Monday to Friday, 7:00 AM - 4:00 PM or 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Qualifications

  • Superior interpersonal and communication skills.
  • Strong moral fortitude and awareness of appropriate behavior when supporting customers.
  • A passion for educating and inspiring customers about Tesla’s energy products.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to identify customer needs quickly.
